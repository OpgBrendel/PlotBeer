Our Team

Plot is nothing without an as well balanced team as our beer is !  We consist of 3 beer lovers; Naomi, Diana and Olivier. Together we make sure every personality can and will enjoy a spectacular day whether you drink in a plot in the sun in a par or while bungeejumping of the tallest building in The Netherlands.


Our Brewery

Our brewery is located in the beautiful island of Curacao. Here are lots of locals working with passion to make sure you taste the love of Plot!


Vision and Mission 

In a society where individuals fall prey to pre-defined routines and standards, we believe that people should cross their limits by seizing any chance that comes their way, because only then we can grasp life's endless opportunities. Our mission is to encourage people to embrace spontaneity and live their life to the fullest!
