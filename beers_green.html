The Beer 
Brewing and ingredients 

Brewed locally on the beautiful island of Curacao, your favourite Plot beer is made from sustainably sourced ingredients. We honour the traditions of local brew crafters and cherish their passion, which gives Plot beers just the right taste-twist!  

Personality
Green personality

This one is for the peeps with a green personality. Having a personality color green means you are kind, generous and compassionate. Good to have around during a crisis as you remain calm and take control of the situation until it is resolved. You are caring and nurturing to others however, you must be careful not to neglect your own needs while giving to others.
